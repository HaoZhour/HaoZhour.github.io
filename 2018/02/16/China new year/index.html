Posted on 2018-02-16 | In life

More interestingly

More interestingly, when I was taking wine to cashier,he allows me to show my ID to him, I said I didn’t bring ID, he asked me how old are you? I told him I was 26, he didn’t believe me, he said look, a boy of 26. But finally he sold the wine to me. I’m a year older, but why do other people think I’m younger?
Anyway, Then I went home to cook, while my roommate was not at home, he went to work, and I left a bowl of dumplings for him.
